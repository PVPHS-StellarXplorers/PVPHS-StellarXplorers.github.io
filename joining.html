How to Join


We recuit new members at the beginning of each school year. Information is pushed out via the school's daily bulletin and students are welcome to contact us with any additional questions.

The roster is limited to 30 members (36 is potentially possible, but unlikely) and selection is application-based. The application mainly focuses on time-committment and interest in the program; prior knowledge isn't considered.
